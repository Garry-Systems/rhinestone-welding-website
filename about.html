About Us

The welder behind Rhinestone Welding and Machine.

Meet Caleb

Rhinestone Welding and Machine is a mobile stick welding business built on hard work, quality craftsmanship, and showing up when people need it most.

Caleb started welding with a passion for working with his hands and solving problems. What began as hands-on experience turned into a full-service mobile welding operation — bringing professional-grade stick welding directly to customers wherever they are.

Whether it's an emergency repair in the field, a farm gate that needs rebuilding, or a custom fabrication project, Caleb takes pride in delivering solid, reliable welds every time. No job is too small, and every customer gets the same level of care and attention to detail.

  • Stick welding (SMAW)
  • Mobile on-site service
  • Structural steel repairs
  • Farm and ranch equipment
  • Trailer and heavy equipment repair
  • Custom fabrication and builds
  • Emergency / hot shot welding
