About Us

The welder behind Rhinestone Welding and Machine.

Meet Caleb

Rhinestone Welding and Machine is a mobile welding and fabrication business built on hard work, quality craftsmanship, and showing up when people need it most. If you're searching for a mobile welder near you in Nebraska, you found the right one.

Caleb started welding with a passion for working with his hands and solving problems. What began as hands-on experience turned into a full-service mobile welding operation — bringing professional-grade stick welding directly to customers across Fremont, Omaha, Lincoln, and all of Nebraska.

Whether it's an emergency repair in the field, a farm gate that needs rebuilding, or a custom fabrication project, Caleb takes pride in delivering solid, reliable welds every time. No job is too small, and every customer gets the same level of care and attention to detail.

Based out of Fremont, Nebraska, Caleb is willing to travel over 100 miles for a job — got a camper and ain't scared of the road. The phone is on 24/7, and he carries $2 million in liability insurance so every job is fully covered.
