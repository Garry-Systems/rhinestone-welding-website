Our Services

Professional mobile welding for every job — big or small.

🚚

Mobile Stick Welding

We bring our welding rig directly to your location — based out of Fremont, NE and willing to travel 100+ miles for a job. Whether it's in the field, on the job site, or at your property, there's no need to transport heavy equipment to a shop. Fully covered with $2 million in liability insurance.

⚡

Hot Shot / Emergency Welding

Equipment breakdowns don't wait for business hours — and neither do we. The phone is on 24/7. Emergency job availability may vary based on time of night and weather, but the call always gets answered. We'll work with you to get it handled.

🏗

Structural Repairs

From steel beams and columns to structural frameworks, we provide strong, reliable welds for structural repair work. Safety and quality are our top priorities on every structural job.

🌾

Farm & Ranch Welding

We understand the demands of agricultural work. Gates, fences, feeders, livestock panels, and other farm equipment — we repair and build what you need to keep your operation running smoothly.

🔧

Equipment & Trailer Repair

Cracked frames, broken hitches, worn-out joints — we repair trailers, implements, and heavy equipment so you can get back to work without the downtime.

⚙

Custom Fabrication

Got a project in mind? We design and fabricate custom parts, brackets, mounts, and more. If you can describe it, we can build it — tailored to your exact specifications.
